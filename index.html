Greatest Rap Groups Ever

Ranking Criteria

1. Multiple major albums/hits

2. Longevity > 7 years

3. Superstars outside from the group

NWA

The impact from this group is undeniable and, initially, undefined.

Their track list is a historical playlist of LA influenced anthems.

Most notable stars are: Ice Cube, Dr. Dre, and Eazy E

OutKast

Their contribution to music is justified by the influx of so many Southern rappers.

A rap sheet that spans some 20 years of head bobbing rhythms and beats.

With just 2 star players, BigBoi and Andre 3000, they did great for the South

A Tribe Called Quest

Classic hits from the award winning album Low End Theory and others.

They influenced a new breed of Hip Hop emcee.

Although Phife Dawg is gone, their other leading member, Q-tip, is still relevant.
